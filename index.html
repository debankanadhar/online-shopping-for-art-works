Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if the artwork is authentic?
We work directly with artists or reputable galleries to ensure the authenticity of all artworks. Additionally, certificates of authenticity may be provided with purchases.

What if the artwork looks different in person than it does online?
We strive to provide accurate representations of artworks through high-quality images and detailed descriptions. However, if you're not satisfied with your purchase, we offer a hassle-free return policy.
How is shipping handled for artworks?
We partner with trusted shipping carriers to ensure that your artwork is carefully packaged and delivered safely to your doorstep. Shipping costs and estimated delivery times are provided during the checkout process.
What if my artwork arrives damaged?
While we take every precaution to ensure that your artwork arrives in pristine condition, accidents can happen during transit. If your artwork arrives damaged, please contact us immediately, and we'll arrange for a replacement or refund.
Can I return or exchange an artwork if it doesn't fit my space or if I change my mind?
Yes, we understand that buying art online can be daunting, which is why we offer a flexible return and exchange policy. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return or exchange it within a specified period.
